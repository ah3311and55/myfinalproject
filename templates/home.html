code for palestine

Code for Palestine was born in 2015 out of a desire to teach coding and entrepreneurial
skills to some of the brightest high school students throughoutthe West Bank and Gaza Strip.
The organization funding the initiative,10th grade and concluding after 12th grade.
Each year has many in-person sessions but is kicked off by a two-week camp held
during the summer. Volunteer undergraduate instructors from Stanford University
have served as the primary teachers during the two-week summer camp.
